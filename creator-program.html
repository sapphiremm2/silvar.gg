Views are counted based on unique viewers who see your content with our referral link or discount code. We use analytics from your platform plus our own tracking system.

What kind of content should I create?

Anything related to Roblox gaming! Popular content includes: unboxing videos, item reviews, trading guides, gameplay highlights, and marketplace comparisons.
